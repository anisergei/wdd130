History

White Water Rafting was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts. Since then, we have been leading the way in providing safe and exhilarating rafting experiences. White water rafting is a recreational outdoor activity that involves navigating a raft through rough or turbulent water, often on a river. It has become a popular adventure sport since the 1950s, if not earlier, and has evolved from individuals paddling small rafts with double-bladed paddles or oars to multi-person rafts propelled by single-bladed paddles and steered by a person at the stern or by the use of oars.https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rafting
